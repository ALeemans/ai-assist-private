# Register for Kraamzorg (Maternity Care)

Contact and register with a kraamzorg organization. This needs to be done early as they can get booked up. Kraamzorg provides essential postnatal care at home for the first week after birth.

**Actions:**
- Research kraamzorg organizations in your area
- Compare services and reviews
- Register with chosen organization
- Confirm coverage with insurance

**Important**: Book early - popular organizations fill up months in advance!
