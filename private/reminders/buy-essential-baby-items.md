# Buy Essential Baby Items - Car Seat Priority

Start purchasing essential baby items, with car seat being the top priority (required before leaving hospital). Also get crib, stroller, and other major items.

**Must-have before birth:**
- Car seat (autostoeltje) - legally required for hospital discharge
- Crib/bed with mattress
- Newborn clothes and sleeping bags
- Diapers and wipes

**Can shop now:**
- Stroller/pram
- Baby monitor
- Changing table
