**Dutch Parental Leave (2026) - Quick Reference:**
- Geboorteverlof: First week after birth (100% paid)
- Partnerverlof: Up to 5 weeks (70% paid, first week 100%)
- Ouderschapsverlof: 26x weekly work hours per parent (unpaid, unless employer compensates)
